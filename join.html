WHY JOIN TEAM 11348?

Discover the incredible benefits and opportunities that await you as a member of our robotics team.

STEM Education

Gain hands-on experience in engineering, programming, and design that goes beyond classroom learning.

Competition Experience

Compete in exciting FIRST Robotics competitions and showcase your skills on a national stage.

Teamwork & Leadership

Develop essential collaboration and leadership skills that will benefit you in college and career.

Networking

Connect with industry professionals, college recruiters, and like-minded students passionate about STEM.

Career Preparation

Build a strong foundation for future careers in engineering, technology, and innovation fields.

Community Impact

Participate in outreach events and inspire the next generation of scientists and engineers.

TEAM ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES

Find your place on our team with various roles that match your interests and skills.

Mechanical Team

Build & Design

  • CAD design and 3D modeling
  • Robot fabrication and assembly
  • Mechanism development
  • Prototyping and testing

Skills: CAD, machining, problem-solving

Programming Team

Code & Automation

  • Robot control systems
  • Autonomous programming
  • Sensor integration
  • Vision processing

Skills: Java, Python, algorithms

Electrical Team

Power & Control

  • Wiring and circuit design
  • Power distribution
  • Sensor installation
  • Electrical troubleshooting

Skills: Electronics, soldering, safety

Business & Outreach

Marketing & Finance

  • Sponsorship acquisition
  • Budget management
  • Social media and PR
  • Community events

Skills: Communication, organization

Design & Media

Creative & Visual

  • Team branding
  • Competition pit design
  • Photography/videography
  • Website and graphics

Skills: Design, creativity, media

Strategy & Scouting

Analysis & Planning

  • Game strategy development
  • Competition analysis
  • Alliance selection
  • Driver practice coordination

Skills: Analysis, planning, teamwork

HOW TO JOIN

Follow these simple steps to become a member of Team 11348.

1

Attend Info Session

Come to one of our information sessions to learn about the team and meet current members.

2

Submit Application

Complete our online application form to express your interest and tell us about yourself.

3

Interview

Meet with team leaders for a brief interview to discuss your interests and availability.

4

Orientation

Attend new member orientation and get started with your chosen subteam.
