OUR SUBTEAMS

Specialized groups that work together to make our robotics program successful.

MECHANICAL

Designs and builds the physical robot structure, mechanisms, and drive systems using CAD and fabrication tools.

PROGRAMMING

Develops software for robot autonomy, teleoperation, and vision processing using Java and Python.

ELECTRICAL

Manages wiring, sensors, motor controllers, and power distribution for reliable robot operation.

DESIGN & MEDIA

Creates team branding, competition pit design, promotional materials, and documentation.

BUSINESS & OUTREACH

Manages sponsorships, community engagement, fundraising, and team promotion.

STRATEGY & DRIVERS

Develops game strategies, practices driving, and competes with the robot during matches.
