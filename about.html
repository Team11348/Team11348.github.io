ABOUT TEAM 11348

Learn about our journey, mission, and the values that drive us to excel in the FIRST Robotics Competition.

Our Story

Team 11348 was founded in 2025 with a vision to inspire young minds to pursue careers in STEM fields through hands-on robotics experience. What started as a small group of passionate students and mentors has grown into a thriving community of innovators.

Over the next years, we plan to compete in numerous FIRST Robotics Competitions, earning recognition for both our technical achievements and our commitment to community outreach. Our team members have gone on to pursue engineering, computer science, and other STEM-related fields at top universities.

We believe that robotics is more than just building machines—it's about developing problem-solving skills, fostering teamwork, and creating lasting connections that extend far beyond the competition season.

OUR MISSION & VISION

Guiding principles that shape everything we do as a team.

Our Mission

To inspire students to become leaders in STEM fields by providing hands-on experience in engineering, programming, and business through competitive robotics.

Our Vision

To create a world where every student has the opportunity to discover their potential through hands-on STEM education and collaborative problem-solving.

Our Values

We value Gracious Professionalism®, cooperation, innovation, and inclusion. We believe that learning should be fun, challenging, and accessible to all.
