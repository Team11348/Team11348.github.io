FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick answers to common questions about our team and programs.

How can I join the team?

We accept new members at the beginning of each school year. Fill out our interest form or attend one of our information sessions to learn more.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is required! We welcome students of all skill levels and provide training in all areas of robotics.

What is the time commitment?

During build season (January-February), we meet 15-20 hours per week. Off-season, we meet 6-8 hours per week.

How can I sponsor the team?

We offer various sponsorship levels. Contact our business team to discuss partnership opportunities and benefits.

Can I volunteer as a mentor?

Yes! We're always looking for mentors in engineering, programming, business, and other fields. No robotics experience necessary.

Where do you compete?

We compete in regional FIRST Robotics Competitions and aim to qualify for the World Championship each year.
