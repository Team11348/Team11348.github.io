TEAM 11348: INNOVATORS OF TOMORROW

Welcome to FRC Team 11348, where we combine engineering excellence with creative problem-solving to compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition. Our team of dedicated students builds robots that push the boundaries of innovation.

OUR CORE VALUES

At Team 11348, we're committed to developing technical skills, fostering teamwork, and inspiring the next generation of innovators.

Engineering Excellence

We design and build competitive robots using cutting-edge technology and engineering principles.

Teamwork & Collaboration

Our diverse team works together to solve complex challenges and achieve our common goals.

STEM Education

We're passionate about promoting science, technology, engineering, and math in our community.
